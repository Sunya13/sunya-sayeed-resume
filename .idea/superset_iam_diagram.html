Key IAM Principles Illustrated

Mapped Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Superset's internal roles (Alpha/Gamma) are dynamically mapped from the user's corporate AD Group claims passed via PingFederate, ensuring enterprise roles drive application privileges.

Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC)

The user's environment-based roles are configured via policies that examine multiple attributes (the AD Groups contained in the JWT), enabling flexible, policy-driven authorization at the application layer.

Granular Authorization (Least Privilege)

Access is controlled at the lowest level possible (widget-level, dashboard level, or data layer), ensuring the user's computed role limits their view strictly to necessary resources.

Identity Federation

The system utilizes OAuth 2.0/OIDC (with PingFederate) to delegate Authentication, centralizing credential management and securing the **first mile** of access.
